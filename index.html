Anything challenging is peeks my interest. That is why I enjoy mind challenging games. Competing against others makes the games more enjoyable for me. It also provides bragging rights. Some of my favorite games include Pente and scrabble. Othello is also on that list. then There is video games. Video games are in a seperate category all by themselves. They are one of the most competitive and mind challenging things I have involved myself in. They are also very addictive.
